Student-Powered Entrepreneurship

RCMakes is a school-based, student-operated enterprise at Rancho Campana High School in Camarillo. We run a community makerspace & contract job shop, do video production and software development, offer expanded learning workshops, host events and offer a number of products for sale.

We produce goods and services, provide internships and student employment, award earned academic scholarships, and incubate startup businesses.
